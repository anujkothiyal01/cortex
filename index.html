Frequently Asked Questions

What is an 'agentic AI application'?

An agentic AI application is a system powered by AI models that can perceive its environment, make decisions, and take autonomous actions to achieve specific goals. Unlike traditional AI, it's proactive and can handle complex, multi-step tasks without constant human intervention.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but a typical project follows our four-step process. A proof-of-concept can take 4-6 weeks, while a full-scale deployment may range from 3 to 6 months. We provide a detailed timeline after the initial discovery phase.

What industries do we specialize in?

While our solutions are industry-agnostic, we have deep expertise in finance, e-commerce, healthcare, and logistics. We are always excited to tackle challenges in new domains and adapt our technology to fit unique industry needs.
